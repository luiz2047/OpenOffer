# Refund Policy

_Last updated: June 16, 2026_

OpenOffer is free and open-source software. The public build does not sell subscriptions, paid licenses, hosted quota, or paid trials.

Because there is no OpenOffer checkout in the public build, there is no OpenOffer refund flow.

If you paid a third party for packaging, support, consulting, hosting, or a custom build, contact that seller directly. Their refund policy applies to that separate transaction.
